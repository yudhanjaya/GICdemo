Conducting Examinations

![/gic/images/pdf_button.png](/gic/images/pdf_button.png)/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?task=pdf&id=2118

In addition to the examinations relevant to training programs conducted by NILS,

on request of State, Semi-Government and Private Sector Organizations,

examination are conducted in a professional manner for recruitments, efficiency

bar and   promotions. Assuring confidentiality and high degree of professional

standards in conducting examinations of any nature and releasing of results

within a reasonable time frame are key features maintained in conducting

examinations by the Institute. NILS posses infrastructure facilities for

comfortably conducting examinations up to 300 candidates.
