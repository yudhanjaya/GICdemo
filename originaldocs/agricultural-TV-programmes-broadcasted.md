Farmers, students, teachers

Process of requesting

Request by letter with blank CDs/ DVDs to the National Agriculture Information

and Communication Centre (NAICC) with information of the film broadcasted or

refer the brochure of video films produced by NAICC

Free for the departmental officers and Rs5/= per minute for duplicating to

others.

Time required

Within 2 weeks.
