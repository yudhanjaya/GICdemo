Buying dairy and animal products

![/gic/images/pdf_button.png](/gic/images/pdf_button.png)/gic/index.php/en/component/info/?task=pdf&id=816

Qualifications

Compulsory For All

Related only for the selling of fresh milk.

A deposit should be submitted.

A photograph of the selling area should be produced along with the application.

Registration is done after an inspection of the place and only if it is in

proper condition.(as a seller and a distributor)

A fast registration certificate is issued.

Process of Submitting the Application

(From where to obtain applications, where to forward, counter and time)

The relevant information could be obtained from the office during office

hours,over the phone and through newspapers.

Places where the Applications can be obtained

Applications can be obtained from the Head office in Narahenpita.

Time allocated for Forwarding

Head office in Narahenpita – during week days,8.30am – 4.15pm

Fees Payable for Obtaining Services

Type of charging Charge for Ordinary Service Charge for Priority Service Selling

of dairy and animal products(only a deposit should be submitted) - No Priority

service

Duration taken to provide Services-Ordinary & Priority Services

Ordinary service- varies according to the waiting list

Priority service-no

Proof of Documents required

Letter with the attached photograph of the place–Original copy
