## Apply for Approval to Transfer Raw Materials or Accessories Between BOI Approved Projects

If you have BOI (Board of Investment) approved projects, you can apply for approval to transfer raw materials or accessories between them. This guide explains the process to get this approval.

To transfer raw materials or accessories, both the importer and the consignee must have signed agreements with the BOI and obtained approval to import the materials.

## Important Information

- There is no fee charged for the approval by the Exports Unit.
- The Imports Unit charges a fee of Rs. 385.00 for the approval.
- The approval process takes approximately 10 minutes.

## What You Need

To apply for the approval, you'll need to provide the following documents:

1. Invoice
2. No objection letters from both projects
3. Copies of Import CUSDECS and Invoices
4. Order form

## How to Apply

1. Obtain the application form from the BOI website (www.investsrilanka.com).
2. Submit the completed application form and required documents to the Exports Unit of the BOI.
   - The office is open from 8:30 AM to 4:15 PM on public working days.
3. The Exports Unit and Imports Unit will review your application and provide the approval.
