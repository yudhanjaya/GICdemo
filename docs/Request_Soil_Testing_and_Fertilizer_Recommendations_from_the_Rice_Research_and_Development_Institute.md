## Request Soil Testing and Fertilizer Recommendations from the Rice Research and Development Institute

The Rice Research and Development Institute (RRDI) offers soil testing services to help researchers, students, and farmers identify soil problems and get recommendations for the right fertilizers to use. This service is available nationwide.

## Important Information

- There is no cost for the soil testing service.
- You must get prior permission before submitting soil samples.
- The testing and recommendations are provided free of charge.

## What You Need

To request the soil testing service, you will need to provide:

- A letter, fax, or email requesting the soil testing
- Soil samples from the area you want tested

The RRDI staff can advise you on how to properly collect the soil samples.

## Step-by-Step Process

1. Request the soil testing service by sending a letter, fax, or email to the RRDI.
2. Receive approval from the RRDI to submit your soil samples.
3. Collect the soil samples as instructed by the RRDI staff.
4. Submit the soil samples to the RRDI.
5. The RRDI will test the soil and provide you with a report identifying any problems and recommending the appropriate fertilizers.

## Get Help

If you need assistance with requesting the soil testing service or have any other questions:

- Call the RRDI at +94 37 225 9881 from 8:30 AM to 4:15 PM, Monday to Friday.
- Visit the RRDI office in Bathalagoda, Ibbagamuwa.
- Email the RRDI at rice@sltnet.lk. Responses typically take 2 working days.
