## Pay Traffic Fines and Retrieve Your Driving License

If you are caught breaking a traffic rule, the police will confiscate your driving license and issue you a spot fine statement. To get your license back, you need to pay the fine and follow the steps below.

## Important Information

- You must pay the fine within 14 days to avoid being summoned to court.
- The fine amount is listed on the spot fine statement issued by the police.
- You can pay the fine at any post office during their operating hours.

## What You Need

- Spot fine statement issued by the police
- Cash or method of payment to pay the fine

## Step-by-Step Process

1. Visit the police station that issued the spot fine statement and receive a fine payment form from the Traffic Division.
2. Go to the nearest post office and pay the fine. You will receive a paid receipt.
3. Return to the police station and submit the paid receipt. The police will then return your driving license.
